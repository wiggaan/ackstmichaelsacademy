ABOUT US

ACK St. Michaels and All Angels Academy is an upcoming Christian-based school located in Banana Hill, Kiambu (opposite Family Bank). We nurture children from Playgroup, Daycare, Grade One and Two, building a strong foundation in academics, faith, and life skills.

Our Mission

To provide a Christ-centered education that nurtures academic excellence, moral values, and holistic growth for every child.

Our Vision

To raise God-fearing, knowledgeable, and responsible young learners who positively impact their community and the world.
